**Grocery Price Comparison Dashboard – Power BI**

**Executive Summary**

This project analyzes grocery product prices across multiple retail stores to identify pricing differences and help consumers or businesses make cost-effective purchasing decisions. The dashboard compares the average prices of common grocery items such as bread, rice, eggs, apples, milk, and chicken across FreshCo, Costco, No Frills, and Walmart. By visualizing price differences at both the store level and product level, the dashboard highlights which stores are generally more affordable and which products show the highest variation in pricing.

**Business Problem**

Grocery prices often vary significantly across stores, making it difficult for consumers to determine where they can get the best value for everyday essentials.

The key business questions addressed in this dashboard are:

Which grocery store offers the lowest average prices?

Which store appears most expensive overall?

Which products have the highest price variation across stores?

Are certain stores consistently cheaper for specific items?

This analysis helps support smarter purchasing decisions, price benchmarking, and competitive retail analysis.

**Methodology**

The dashboard was created in Power BI using a grocery pricing dataset containing products and store level prices.

**Steps followed**

**Data Preparation**

Imported grocery pricing data into Power BI

Validated store names and product categories

Checked for missing or inconsistent price values

**Data Transformation**

Used Power Query to format product and store fields.

Standardized pricing values for consistent comparison.

Prepared the data model for slicer based filtering.

**Visualization Development**

Built slicers for product and store.

Created a bar chart to compare average price by store.

Added a clustered column chart for product-level store comparison.

Designed the layout for quick decision-making and easy filtering.

**Insight Generation**

Compared overall store pricing trends.

Identified store specific price advantages by product.

Highlighted products with the largest cost differences.

**Results**

The dashboard reveals several practical insights:

Walmart appears to have the lowest average grocery prices overall.

FreshCo and Costco show comparatively higher average prices at the store level.

Product-level comparison shows that bread and rice have noticeable price differences across stores.

Some products, such as eggs and apples, show more balanced pricing between retailers.

The slicer based analysis makes it easy to identify the most affordable store for each selected product
